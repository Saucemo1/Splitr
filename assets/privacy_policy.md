# Privacy Policy for Splitr

**Last updated: January 6, 2025**

## Introduction

Splitr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Library Access
- We access your device's camera and photo library to scan receipts and bills
- Images are processed locally on your device and sent to Google's Gemini AI service for text extraction
- We do not store or retain any images after processing

### Usage Data
- We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app
- This includes app crashes, feature usage, and performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

- **Receipt Processing:** Images are sent to Google's Gemini AI service solely for text extraction
- **App Functionality:** Processed text is used to create bill splits and calculations
- **App Improvement:** Anonymous usage data helps us improve the app experience

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties
- Images are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for processing but are not stored by Google
- We may share anonymous usage statistics with analytics providers

## Data Security

- All data transmission is encrypted
- Images are processed and immediately discarded
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google's Gemini AI service for receipt text extraction. Please review Google's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

## Your Rights

- You can revoke camera and photo library access at any time in your device settings
- You can delete the app at any time to stop data collection
- Contact us if you have questions about your data

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
